General Terms and Conditions

  • before the settlement a superficial inspection of the animals and a mandatory check of the veterinary passport for all necessary vaccinations

  • animals without a veterinary passport or with pronounced aggression towards people are not admitted to the hotel

  • vaccination not earlier than 21 days and not later than 1 year before settling in the hotel

  • deworming not later than 45 days, treatment against ectoparasites not earlier than 3 days

  • a prepayment of one day's stay at the hotel (cash or bank card) is required for booking

  • if the booking is cancelled later than 5 days before the check-in date, the booking fee will not be refunded
